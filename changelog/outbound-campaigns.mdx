<Update label="August 8th, 2025">
  ## Outbound Maintenance Texting
  
  <Badge variant="secondary">Author: Team</Badge>
  
  Goal-based texting campaigns for maintenance and follow-ups:
  - **Batch Text Campaigns** - Send 2-way bookable texts in controlled batches
  - **Goal-Based Progression** - Check campaign goals before sending next batch
  - **Smart Scheduling** - Configure optimal send times for your audience
  - **Retry via Call** - Fallback to phone calls for non-responsive text recipients
  - **Performance Tracking** - Monitor batch performance and goal achievement
  
  Successfully demoed with Golden Rule on August 6th.
</Update>
