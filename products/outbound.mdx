### How It Works
1. **Lead submits form** on your website or third-party platform
2. **Instant trigger** to Avoca's AI system
3. **Personalized call** placed within 30 seconds
4. **Qualification** and appointment booking in one call

## Campaign Types

### Happy Calls
Automated follow-up calls after service completion:
- Check satisfaction with the service
- Address any concerns immediately
- Request reviews from happy customers
- Identify upsell opportunities

### Maintenance Reminders
Proactive outreach for recurring services:
- Annual HVAC tune-ups
- Filter replacements
- System inspections
- Warranty reminders

### Custom Campaigns
Design your own outreach campaigns:
- Seasonal promotions
- Service area expansions
- New service announcements
- Customer win-back initiatives
