## Overview

This demo shows how the AI agent makes an outbound call to inform a customer about needing to reschedule their appointment due to high service volume, and successfully gets their agreement.

## Key Highlights

- **Proactive Communication**: Calls ahead rather than no-show
- **Clear Explanation**: Honest about high volume reason
- **Apologetic Tone**: Shows consideration for inconvenience
- **Successful Outcome**: Customer accepts rescheduling
