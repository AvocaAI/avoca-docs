## Overview

This demo shows how the AI agent handles a customer who decides to cancel their dryer vent cleaning booking after learning the price has increased significantly from their previous service.

- **Respects Decision**: Doesn't pressure when customer cancels
- **Professional Closure**: Offers help with other services
