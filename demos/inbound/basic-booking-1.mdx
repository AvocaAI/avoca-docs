## Overview

This demo showcases how the AI agent seamlessly handles a warm transfer from Home Depot, manages the transition when the Home Depot rep exits the call, and successfully books an appointment while handling scheduling objections.

<Note>
  You may hear beeps and skips in the demo calls, as they were used to remove
  PII of customers.
</Note>

## Key Highlights

- **Warm Transfer Handling**: Agent smoothly manages the handoff from Home Depot
- **Professional Transition**: Maintains professionalism when third party exits
- **Objection Handling**: Effectively manages scheduling concerns
- **Complete Booking**: Successfully schedules the appointment
