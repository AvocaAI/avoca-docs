  ## Customer Journey Mapping
  
  Visualize and understand every customer interaction across their entire lifecycle. Our new Customer Journey feature maps all touchpoints to give you a complete view of each customer's experience:
  
  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/customer-journey.png" alt="Customer Journey Mapping" />
  </Frame>
  
  - **Timeline View** - See every call, text, email, and chat in chronological order
  - **Interaction Analytics** - Understand sentiment and outcomes at each touchpoint
  - **Journey Stages** - Track customers from first contact through loyalty
  - **Drop-off Analysis** - Identify where customers disengage and why
  - **Predictive Insights** - AI predicts next best actions for each customer
  
  This holistic view enables you to optimize every stage of the customer experience and identify opportunities to improve satisfaction and revenue.

<Update label="Q4 2025">
  ## Revenue Intelligence via Integration
  
  Deep integration with your CRM and field service management tools to unlock powerful revenue insights:
  
  - **Conversation-to-Revenue Tracking** - Connect every customer interaction directly to closed deals
  - **Win/Loss Analysis** - AI analyzes why deals close or fall through based on communication patterns
  - **Revenue Forecasting** - Predict monthly and quarterly revenue based on pipeline activity
  - **ROI by Channel** - Understand which communication channels drive the most revenue
  
  ## Lead Pipelines
  
  Transform how you manage and nurture leads with visual pipeline management:
  
  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/lead-pipeline.png" alt="Lead Pipeline Management" />
  </Frame>
  
  - **Visual Pipeline Stages** - Drag-and-drop leads through customizable stages
  - **Automated Lead Scoring** - AI scores leads based on interaction quality and intent
  - **Smart Follow-up Sequences** - Automated nurture campaigns based on lead behavior
  - **Pipeline Velocity Tracking** - Monitor how quickly leads move through stages
  - **Conversion Analytics** - Understand what actions drive conversions at each stage
